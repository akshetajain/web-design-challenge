Summary of Project

We utilized a data set comprising of over 500 cities to explore the relationship between weather and geopositioning. How does weather change as we get closer to the equator?

Our extensive data covered over 500 cities. We looked at the following factors: temperature, cloudiness, wind speed, and humidity. This website is designed to easily read the results of our study. This includes the source data, visualizations, explanations, and descriptions of any trends and correlations.
